Ceaser salad

Ever wanted to discover the perfect Caesar salad recipe?
Here is a recipe for a Caesar salad that is easy to prepare and the most delicious you can make or taste:
fresh and fresh lettuce leaves with garlic, mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce, large and pampering croutons and lots of Grana Padano cheese (or Parmesan).
calories: 439 per serving, 112 calories per 100 grams.



To watch on YouTube: Ceaser salad.

How to prepare the recipe:

1. Tear the lettuce into coarse pieces by hand and place in a deep bowl.
2. Mix all the sauce ingredients well and pour over the lettuce in a bowl.
3. Add over the croutons and Grana Padano cheese chips.
4.Serve immediately Caesar salad is easy to prepare, special and delicious not to be missed.

Orange lentil soup

Delicious winter soup with orange lentils and vegetables.
a soup that is worth adopting during the winter because it is easy to prepare, nourishes and warms the throat and heart.
calories: 145 per serving, 38 calories per 100 grams.



To watch on YouTube: Orange lentil soup.

How to prepare the recipe:

1. Cut the carrots, potatoes, onions and sweet potatoes into small cubes and fry them in oil for a few minutes.
2. Add the boiling water and cook the vegetables for about 15 minutes.
3. Add the orange lentils, chopped parsley and spices, mix and cook covered for about 15 minutes until the vegetables and lentils soften.
4. When the soup cools down a bit, grind it in a rod blender (you can set aside some of the chopped vegetables that are not ground and add to the soup after grinding).
5.Serve with soup almonds or croutons.
